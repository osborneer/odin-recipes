Tacos

Description

This recipe is for classic ground beef tacos in a soft tortilla. These tacos are highly customizable, as you can add any toppings you want! We recommend shredded lettuce, shredded cheese, sliced avocado, sour cream, and Tapatio hot sauce!

Ingredients

  • One pound of ground beef
  • One packet of taco seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Brown the beef in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Drain the fat from the beef and return it to the pan.
  3. Stir in the packet of taco seasoning and the 1/4 cup of water.
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low and let simmer, stirring occasiaonally.
  5. Serve with toppings of your choice!
