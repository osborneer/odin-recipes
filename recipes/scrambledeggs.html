Scrambled Eggs

Description

This recipe is for a easy scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Two eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • One tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until beaten well. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet. Cook until eggs are firm, but still moist, stirring occassionaly.
